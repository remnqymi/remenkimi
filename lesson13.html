Lesson =ij   13  The Conjunctions
Generally we can say that the article ovoh is used to join sentences and verbs, but the article nem is used to join adjectives and nouns.

Examples:
ovoh    And       (Conjuncting verbs)

`N0o4 a4goc ovoh a42wpi.
He   said  and  it was.

`N0o4 a4honhen ovoh avcwnt .
He ordered and it was created.

iPi`cbwov`i a4w2 ovoh a4`cqai `mpiw2.
The pupil read and  wrote the lesson.

Conjuncting sentences

ovoh matovbon ebolha 0wleb niben
and purify us from all uncleanliness.

nem And (Conjuncting nouns)

Anok pe 5anactacic nem piwnq
I am the resurrection and life.

`Cmov ero4 pir3 nem piioh
Praise Him O Sun and Moon.

Iwakim nem Anna
Yoakim and Anna
