We can learn from the previous simple discription of the Bible that there are 3 types of articles used in the Coptic Language:
pi     `p    `f       for masculine singular
5     `t    `0        feminine singular
ni    nen             plural

There is no major difference between the use of any of the above articles other than that `fand `0can only be used if the word begins with one of the following letters:
    b    i    l    m    n    ov    r

Examples:
`fbwk
 The Servant
`0baki
 The City
`fiwt
 The Father
`0mav
 The Mother 
`fran
 The Name
`0negi
 The Womb 
i.e. you can use pirwmi instead of `frwmi (The Man)
piran `fran (The Name)

+ The exceptions are that (The Father) is always `fiwtbecause piiwtmeans barley and also`piwt is an excpetion to differentiate between barley and father. There are some other rare exceptions and are all for differentiation between words.
