Lesson 11  i=a   The Indefinite Articles
 
There are two indefinite articles used in the Coptic Language:
Ov (a, an) singular (masculine and feminine) 
Han (some) plural (masculine and feminine) 

Examples:
Ovdia03k3 a covenant 
Ovalov a youth 
Ovh3t a heart 
Ovehoov a day 
Oviab an elephant 
     
Hanslil (some) offerings 
Hanwili rams 
Hanmaci bulls 
Han2f3r friends
